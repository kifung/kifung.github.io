About Me

Hi there! I'm currently a senior majoring in Computer Science at UC Santa Barbara. I'm from San Francisco, California & I love finding the best places to eat wherever I go... especially ice cream.

I am also passionate about empowering others to learn CS, so my peers and I started Womxn/Hacks, an all female-identifying hackathon at UCSB last year! This year, I was honored to receieve a scholarship to attend the Grace Hopper Conference 2019 through my university. Since it will be my first time attending the conference, I am super excited to go (!!!) and would love to connect with you if you're attending too!
